## Possessive suffix

In English, when something belongs to someone, it is _someone's_ something. We add _'s_ to the possessing noun.

In Lugso, similarly, the cultist adds **-gu**.

**vik ungu...**

`priest 3SG-POSS`

_Its priest..._

---

**uyyib lukgu...**

`eye-PL 2SG-POSS`

_Your eyes..._

---

**hupz kurgu...**

`human 1SG-POSS`

_My human..._

---

**tkuy isvgu...**

`future heretic-POSS`

_The/A heretic's future..._
